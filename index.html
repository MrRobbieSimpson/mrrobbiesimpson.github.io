I’m Rob Simpson, a 30 year old Designer based in Belfast. I’m a graduate from the Belfast School of Art (Ulster University).

Throughout my career, I’ve gone on to work for some renowned teams such as InCrowd Sports, AuditComply, Belfast Metropolitan College and UNRVLD. I focus on Visual Design but I dip my toe in some Front-End Development as well as Motion Graphics on the side.

Biography

I took an interest in design after choosing my first college course. In this course, we were introduced to the Adobe Creative Suite which we used for web development and graphic design. Since realising the potential of Photoshop and Illustrator I followed tutorials online & read magazines to teach myself the fundamentals of these programs & learn new skills.
